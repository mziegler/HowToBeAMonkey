About

Nearly every day, researchers at the Lomas Barbudal Monkey Project follow groups of wild capuchin monkeys through a tropical dry forest in Costa Rica to observe their behavior. Since Dr. Susan Perry started studying this population of monkeys in 1990, researchers have been collecting many different types of data about the monkeys, including their foraging and social behaviors, hormone levels (from fecal samples) and genetics. This detailed long-term observation of these wild monkeys helps us understand the evolution of primate intelligence and social behavior.

Winslow Homer is a baby monkey in one of the project's study groups. Some of the researchers followed Winslow Homer all day on January 24, 2014, and you can see some of the data that they collected on this map. You can get a glimpse into the life of a baby capuchin monkey, and learn about some of the data that the Lomas Barbudal Monkey Project collects every day! This map is a work in progress!

Monkey Biographies

Winslow Homer lived in a relatively big social group of 31 monkeys called Abby’s group when the data on this map was collected. (Shortly after, Winslow Homer became the youngest known capuchin male ever to migrate to a new group!)

Abby’s group is named after Abby, the old matriach of the group, when researchers frist started them in 1990 – many of the monkeys in Abby’s group (and all of the highest-ranking females) are decendents of Abby.

Cast of characters

Winslow Homer was born April 8, 2013, to Dali, the alpha female of Abby’s group. He was the darling of the group, widely sought after by would-be babysitters, and exceedingly precocious. He surprised researchers by becoming the youngest male ever to transfer to a new social group, at age 14 months.

Dali was born on July 20, 2001, to Diablita, who was at that time the alpha female of Abby’s group. Researchers generally described her as a “spoiled princess,” as she was always popular with the rest of the group. She always gives the impression of leading a charmed life, weathering all forms of social turmoil and environmental stress as if it were no challenge, while her groupmates are visibly stressed. Dali was the closest ally of her mother Diablita. Dali became alpha female near the end of Diablita’s life.

Vandal, Winslow’s great aunt, is the oldest female in the group (born in 1989). She was, at various times, the closest friend and the chief political rival of Winslow’s grandmother Diablita. Vandal was presumed dead in November 2011 when she vanished for several days, leaving her infant Peeves motherless and wounded. She eventually reappeared, with grisly wounds that still leave her scarred. Researchers hypothesize that she received these wounds while defending her infant from a raptor.

Peeves was born in July 2011 to Winslow’s great-aunt Vandal. His fourth month of life was especially traumatic, when he was wounded and his mother disappeared for several days. But aunt Monster’s loving care enabled him to survive, and he is now a well-adjusted juvenile.

Duende, the current alpha male of the group, was born in 1996 in the neighboring group (Rambo’s group). He migrated to Abby’s group in 2003, along with his brothers Tranquilo and Elmo, and Tranquilo became alpha male. When Tranquilo died in 2007, Duende became alpha male. Duende and Elmo had a fierce struggle for the alpha male position in 2011, in which they alternated between being leader of the group and being a wounded outcast. Duende emerged victorious and Elmo left the group. Duende is a particularly nervous and twitchy alpha male, constantly vigilant and good at running from his enemies to avoid trouble. Researchers are perplexed that he has been able to keep the alpha male position for so long, given his lack of social skills relative to other males.

Tom Jerry migrated into Abby’s group at the end of 2012. He is an excellent politician and constantly tries to ingratiate himself with Dali by offering his babysitting services to Winslow.

Pope John, King James, and King Donkey are all recent (2013) immigrants to the group. Researchers don’t know whether they are related to one another, since they came from outside the study area, but their strong physical resemblance to one another and the timing of their arrival suggests that they are likely to be brothers or cousins.

Winslow’s cousins Frankenstein (Monster’s infant, born 2 months before Winslow Homer) and Dubious (Fishy’s infant born 9 months before Winslow) are Winslow’s most frequent play partners, though he plays often with older juveniles as well. Often Dali had trouble finding Winslow when she wanted to nurse him and would end up nursing baby Frankenstein instead.

Winslow’s uncle Danger and cousins Bilbo, Viceroy and Krishna are the “cool big boys” in the group. Born in 2997-2008, they are young enough to enjoy frequent and quite vigorous rough and tumble play bouts, but old enough to start participating in intergroup encounters and think about leaving home.


Winslow Homer has numerous sisters, cousins and aunts, all of whom are fond of indulging him with grooming, nursing, and cuddling:

Dante was born on December 25, 2004 to Diablita - a then subordinate female in Abby's group. Her father Tranquilo was the group alpha male at the time. Dante currently resides with her maternal half sisters Dali and Seiton plus her full brother Danger and maternal half brother Duplo. She is a very close ally of her sister, Dali, and an important alloparent to Winslow Homer.

Aunt Opie, born in 1993, is a particularly good groomer, when she can spare time from coddling her little son Jigglypuff (born in 2010) and daughter Nickel (born in 2012). Aunts Fishy (born in 1999) and Vladimir (born in 2004), as well as cousins Picasso and Champagne (both born in 2007), are always happy to help Winslow as well, and these female relatives are even willing to give milk to Winslow when his mom isn’t handy.

Cousin Monster was born on June 6, 1995, to Cookie (Vandal’s daughter). Cookie died in Nov 2006, when Monster was just 17 months old and not quite weaned, leaving Monster to be an orphan. She grew up being more of a loner than the other monkeys and was often bullied by her aunts and cousins. Monster lost her first infant shortly before Vandal disappeared from the group. She eagerly adopted baby Peeves while Vandal was away - her care of Peeves during that time probably saved his life. See the story in more detail here:

Other frequent playmates and babysitters include brother Kahlo and aunt Seiton (born in 2009), cousins Quasimono (born 2010) and Arjuna (born 2011) and uncle Duplo (born 2011).

Winslow Homer is a wild baby capuchin monkey who lives in a tropical dry forest in Costa Rica with his big family of 30 other monkeys.

Winslow is Dali the alpha female's baby, so he's quite popular with the other monkeys -- a little prince charming!

SKIP INTRO

This map shows a rather typical day in Winslow Homer's life, with real data collected by scientists.

Every day, Winslow and his big family set out in search of food. Capuchins eat mostly fruit, and they have to spend most of their day travling between food patches -- always on the move, they travel about 3.5 km (2.2 miles) every day, on average.

Scientists have been studying Winslow's family since 1990, to learn about the monkeys' behaviors and understand how they evolved to be so smart.
